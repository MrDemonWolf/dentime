## The Find a Time tab

Click the menu bar icon, then switch the top tab to **Find a Time**.

1. Pick a date and time — in _your_ zone.
2. You’ll see that same moment converted into every friend’s zone.
3. Each row is tinted by what that means locally for them:

| Color     | What it means                                 |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| 🟢 Green  | They’re in working hours — good time to ping  |
| 🟡 Yellow | Edge of day — catch them early or late        |
| 🔴 Red    | They’re probably asleep — pick something else |

## Day-boundary badges

If the meeting happens on a different day in their zone, you’ll see a small `+1d` or `-1d` badge. That’s a lifesaver for Tokyo/London/Chicago schedules.

## Copy as text

Bottom button: **Copy as text**. Drops a one-line summary into your clipboard like:

```
Thursday Apr 24 at 3:00 PM Chicago is 10:00 PM Berlin, 5:00 AM Tokyo
```

Perfect to paste into Slack or Discord.

## Excluding people

Each row has a checkbox. Uncheck anyone you don’t want in the summary. You still see their time — just not in the copy.

## Working hours

DenTime uses **9am–6pm** by default. If your pack keeps weird hours, change it in [Settings → Working hours](/docs/settings).
