## Install

DenTime runs on **macOS 13 Ventura or later**.

1. Open the App Store (coming soon) and search for **DenTime**, or use a direct link from [the homepage](/).
2. Click Get.
3. The app auto-launches after install. Look for the paw icon in your menu bar (top-right of your screen).

<Callout>
	DenTime is a **menu bar app** — it doesn’t appear in your Dock or in Command-Tab. That’s on
	purpose. It’s always one click away from the top of your screen.
</Callout>

## Your first friend

1. Click the paw icon in your menu bar.
2. You’ll see the **Now** tab (your roster).
3. Hit **+ Add friend**.
4. Type their name.
5. Start typing their city in the Timezone box — **Berlin**, **Tokyo**, etc. — and pick from the list.
6. Optionally: pick a group (**Pack**, **Work**, **Family**, or make your own) and a color dot.
7. Add.

Their current time now shows up in your roster. Click their name to copy a ready-to-paste string like:

```
It's 10:42 PM Fri for Alex in Berlin
```
