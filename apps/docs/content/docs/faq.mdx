## Does DenTime collect any data?

**No.** In MVP1, everything stays on your Mac. No account, no servers, no analytics, no crash reporting. The app is Apple-sandboxed and its Privacy Manifest declares zero data collection.

## Does it sync between my devices?

**Not yet.** MVP1 is local-only. Sync (via Apple Sign In + a private Cloudflare Worker API) lands in MVP2. When it does, it’ll be opt-in and we’ll update the Privacy Policy.

## Is there an iPhone app?

**Not yet.** iPhone is MVP2. It’ll share most of the logic with the Mac app.

## Does it export to my calendar?

**Not yet.** iCal / `.ics` export and Apple Calendar integration are MVP2.

## Is it open source?

The docs site lives at [github.com/MrDemonWolf/dentime](https://github.com/MrDemonWolf/dentime). The Swift source is in the same repo under `apps/macOS/`.

## Why a menu bar app and not a regular app?

A timezone tool should be **always available, never in the way**. Menu bar wins. No dock clutter, no Command-Tab noise, one click from the top of your screen.

## How much does it cost?

**Free.** No paywall, no pro tier planned for MVP1 or MVP2.
